# Test Instructions (Reviewer)

1. Go to https://www.cplmanager.com and log in.
2. Open the Transfers page.
3. Use the Now and Pot tabs to apply presets.
4. Confirm badges, borders, and age highlights appear on player cards.
5. Open the extension popup and toggle enhancements on and off.
6. Open Options, change thresholds, and refresh Transfers to see updates.
7. Open the Tryouts page and confirm highlights appear for standout players.
